🎯 Project Mission

Traditional credit scoring systems automatically reject 26 million credit-invisible people — not because they're risky, but because they lack formal credit history. HomeCredit serves these underserved populations using alternative behavioral data.

Our AI pipeline processes 1.5M loan applications across 32 data tables to deliver a fair, explainable, and high-performance default prediction model — exceeding industry AUC benchmarks by 7%.
